Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to MindGuard (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to handling your personal data in strict accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, as well as the rights you have as a data subject.

2. Personal Data Collection and Processing

The App is designed to help you record and manage emotions, thoughts, and cognitive distortions. We adhere to the principle of "privacy by design" and "privacy by default", therefore:

  • Zero Data Collection: The App does not collect, process, or transmit any personal data from users.
  • Local Storage Only: All data you record in the App (including emotions, thoughts, cognitive distortion analysis, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device and never uploaded to any external servers or third-party services.
  • No Device Tracking: The App does not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, operating system information, or any other device-related data.
  • No Behavioral Analysis: We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies to monitor your usage of the App.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, organizations must have a valid legal basis for processing personal data. Since we do not collect or process any personal data, no legal basis is required for our operations. Your data remains entirely under your control on your own device.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device with no network transmission, eliminating the risk of data breaches during transmission.
  • The App utilizes iOS's built-in secure storage mechanisms to ensure your data is encrypted and protected on your device.
  • You retain full control over your data and can delete all records at any time by removing the App from your device.

5. Your Rights under GDPR

As a data subject under the GDPR, you have the following rights, which we fully respect:

  • Right to Be Informed: You have the right to be informed about how your personal data is processed (this policy provides full transparency).
  • Right of Access: You can access all your data directly through the App at any time.
  • Right to Rectification: You can modify or edit any of your recorded data within the App.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete specific records or all data by removing the App.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Since we do not process your data, this right is automatically respected.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your data to other apps or services if you choose to use related features.
  • Right to Object: You can object to any future processing of your data by uninstalling the App.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you believe our processing of your personal data violates the GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not retain any data. You control how long your data is stored by managing it directly on your device.

7. Data Transfers

We do not transfer any data to third parties or across borders, as all data remains on your device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other appropriate means and seek your consent if required by law. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights under the GDPR, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@freemind.app
  • Website: https://www.freemind.app
  • We will respond to your requests within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR requirements.
